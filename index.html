Digital India in 2047

"Digital India 2047" is the vision for transforming India into a developed nation by its 100th year of independence in 2047, a goal often referred to as "Viksit Bharat @2047". The digital transformation is seen as a crucial engine for this vision, focusing on citizen-centric governance, digital inclusion, and leveraging technology like AI, cloud, and IoT to achieve economic, social, and technological growth. Key priorities include bridging the digital divide, strengthening cybersecurity, promoting digital literacy, and developing a strong domestic innovation ecosystem.

About the Vision

Digital India 2047 aims to ensure inclusive growth through technology. It focuses on digital infrastructure, digital platforms for governance and an ecosystem that enables innovation and digital literacy. "Digital India 2047" is the vision for the next phase of India's Digital India program, which aims to guide the country's digital transformation to achieve the goal of a "Viksit Bharat" (Developed India) by the 100th anniversary of independence in 2047. Its key focuses include strengthening digital infrastructure to the last mile, ensuring data privacy and cybersecurity, boosting digital literacy, fostering innovation and a strong domestic tech ecosystem, and leveraging digital technologies to improve governance, education, healthcare, and economic opportunities for all citizens.
